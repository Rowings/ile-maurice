Having first emerged from the ocean 8 million years ago, this volcanic island has been inhabited by a vast majority of the world's cultures; Arab, Malay, Portuguese, Dutch, French, British before declaring its own independence in 1968. Withs its 177km of coastline covered entirely by bright sunny beaches and coral reefs, you are sure to find your pleasure on this tropical paradise.

For many years now Mauritius has been uninvolved in any kind of armed or political conflict, which explains its lack of military and army. Native home of the Dodo bird, you will find peace and relaxation at the foot of the palm trees.
